About us

One platform for homes and apartments, shared your way.

Co-spayze is a platform that helps you find and share both shops and apartments in a simple and comfortable manner. Whether you're looking to share a shared apartment, we connect you with the right spaces and the right people. From discovering, to finding potential roommates all in one place.

At Co-spayze, we believe finding where you belong should feel easy, safe, and right for you.

Why Co-spayze?

We make it easy to find and share the right spaces for living and business.

Affordable

Reduce the cost of rent or shop space by sharing with others, so you don’t have to carry the full financial burden on your own.

Compatible

Find roommates or business partners whose lifestyle, habits, or goals align with yours, making it easier to live or work together comfortably.

Convenient

Easily browse available spaces, connect with the right people, and make decisions faster without the usual stress or endless searching.

Flexible

Whether you need something short term or long term, for living or business, you have options that can adjust to your current situation.
